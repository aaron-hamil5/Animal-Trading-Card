Red Fox

Foxes can sense the Earth's magnetic field, with their eyes! It helps them judge the distance and direction of their pray for pouncing.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes
  • Average Height: 35 - 50 cm
  • Average Speed: 50 km/s
  • Average Mass: 2.2 - 14 kg

A Fox is a small to medium-sized, dog-like mammal with a distinctive, bushy tail and pointed ears. There is over 47 different breeds of fox. These are nocturnal animals, meaning they sleep through the day and active at night, they can live in any continent, just not in Antarctica. The Red Fox is the most common species of fox in the world.
